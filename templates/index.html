About CRSV Observatory

Conflict-Related Sexual Violence (CRSV) is a systematic pattern of abuse used as a weapon of war and a tactic of conflict. The CRSV Observatory is dedicated to documenting these atrocities, understanding their patterns, and supporting global efforts toward accountability and prevention.

Through data visualization, historical analysis, and geographic mapping, we provide researchers, activists, and policymakers with the tools they need to understand and address this grave violation of human rights.
